About Samsara

Welcome to Samsara, where resilience meets altruism in the heart of the chaos. As a premier Private Military Company (PMC), we transcend the conventional labels, akin to an Emergency Medical Service (EMS) of the battlefield. Embracing the spirit of camaraderie and sacrifice, we stand as guardians against the relentless tides of conflict within the world of Helldivers 2.

Mission Statement:

Samsara is more than a PMC; it's a beacon of hope amidst the turmoil. Our unwavering dedication is simple yet profound: to extend a hand to those in need, regardless of the cost. Symbolized by the eternal ouroboros, we epitomize resilience and renewal. In the face of adversity, we rise again and again, ensuring that no ally is left behind.

Our Commitment:

We are pioneers of cutting-edge cloning technology, safeguarding the essence of our members as they navigate the perilous frontlines. Within Samsara, there is no loss without restoration, no sacrifice without redemption. Our alliance extends beyond our ranks, collaborating seamlessly with esteemed divisions like the 66th Armored Division. Whether you seek guidance, support, or sanctuary, know that Samsara stands ready, unwavering in our resolve to aid and uplift all who call upon us.


Join us in the eternal cycle, where every dawn brings renewed purpose and every dusk heralds the triumph of unity. Together, we shall overcome. Together, we are Samsara.

Heroes always get remembered, but legends never die.
